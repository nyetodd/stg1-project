WHAT IS CONTINENTAL DRIFT?

Continental Drift is four great bands, two great labels and two continents meeting on one stellar mini-LP. Frequent collaborators Slumberland Records and Fortuna POP! team up to bring you a smashing eight song sampler of some of the finest pop out there in 2016.

WHO ARE THE BANDS?

Baltimore's WILDHONEY have been making music together since 2011, forging a fresh sound from raw materials of shoegaze, punk and good old indiepop.

East London's TIGERCATS jangly, effervescent pop nods to their punk roots and spikes the pop punch with anthemic choruses and massive, crunching guitars.

Philadelphia's MERCURY GIRLS are one of the most talked-about new bands in ages. Featuring members of Literature, Little Big League and Pet Milk, Mercury Girls have a gorgeous, layered sound that combines post-punk dynamics with soaring/jangling guitars, fantastic melodies and expertly-arranged tunes.

Edinburgh, Scotland's THE SPOOK SCHOOL are surely the most fun band taking-on issues of gender and identity today, writing queer-pop anthems that are noisy, tuneful, sharply-observed and outrageously catchy.

When You Motor Away

Continental Drift offers a collection of songs to make your heart soar and your feet move, like all the best pop music should, and the sheer number of ideas contained in its 8 track, half-hour duration is testament to the way the labels and bands have responded to their self-imposed challenge.

London in Stereo

Continental Drift is bittersweet, serving as both the perfect summer album and a salute to the end of Fortuna POP!.
